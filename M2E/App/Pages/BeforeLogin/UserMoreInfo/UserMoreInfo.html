A Crowd Automation Accepter is a person who answers questions for Crowd Automation. A Crowd Automation Accepter uses our website to find questions, submit answers, and manage his or her account.

CITs - Crowd Individual Tasks :

Each question Requester asks through our application is a Crowd Individual Task, or CIT. A CIT contains all of the information an Accepter needs to answer the question, including information about how the question is shown to the Accepters and what kinds of answers would be considered valid.

Each CIT has a reward, an amount of money Requester pay to the Accepter that successfully completes the CIT.
Requester can request that more than one Accepter to complete a CIT by specifying a quantity of crowdforce required while creating CIT.

From quick and easy CITs like partcipating in survey, moderation, transcription, tagging a picture, to bigger projects like writing and editing articles, there are so many jobs available now..
